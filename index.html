HOW DOES IT WORK

What if it would be possible to leave markers of who we are - for us to be able to look back and understand better where we travelled from. These markers can be put at various points in our lives ; when we graduate to secondary and edge into adolescence, when we graduate to university and edge into adulthood. When we become parent or partner. Or when we look at a short time horizon and want to leave the marker for those who come after us and for whome our history can become part of theirs.

FAQ

At what age should I record?

Obviously anyone who can talk, can talk to the future. But obviously a video “marker” is most relevant and most valuable when we are conscious of who and where we are, but also understand the timeline of life. Whilst everyone's development is paced differently, entering into secondary school is seen as a good moment for recording your first marker, with subsequent ones at different development stages of one's life.

What is the cost?

The first recording will cost 150 USD, with a storage charge of 5 USD/year. Discounts for multiple recordings and/or family discounts are available.

Do I have to store the video in the Talks 2 the Future vault and how secure is it?

After recording and editing, a digital copy will be sent to the client, with one copy safely stored in the Talks 2 the Future video vault. This vault is within the Vimeo app and therefore comes with a long-term guarantee. Access to your stored videos is via the members' login page with individual password protection.

Can I change the interview setting?

For reasons of optimizing the editing process, the interview set-up is standardized (see examples on this website). However, once the recording has been completed, the background can be modified.

What questions and topics are typically covered?

During the first-phase conversation to prepare the interview, a standard set of questions will be provided. These are specifically geared towards covering all important aspects of what makes us who we are: early memories, those we love, our passions and fears, our thoughts, and opinions on topics relevant for the stage of life we are in.

Can I have the interview in another city?

For now, the only physical interview location is Dubai. However, the team is in the process of setting up interview locations in other major cities.
